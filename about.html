About Us

Our Story

Harbor Cellars was founded with a vision to bring the finest wines from around the world to Singapore. Our journey started with a passion for exquisite wine and a dedication to creating luxurious experiences for our clients.

Over the years, we have curated a collection of rare and premium wines, offering personalized services and hosting exclusive wine events for enthusiasts and collectors.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a curated selection of the world's finest wines, paired with exclusive tasting experiences, ensuring luxury, quality, and sophistication in every bottle.

Our Vision

To become Singapore's premier wine distributor, recognized for excellence, exclusive offerings, and elevating wine culture in the region.
